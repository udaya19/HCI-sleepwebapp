About Us

Our Objective

Sleep deprivation is the common problem in today’s world.
Due to lack of sleep in a person he/she can be affected
with many health problems.
Hence to tackle this problem we came up
with a prototype which
enables a person to monitor his/her sleep.

Our Motivation

Through our daily life activities and
busy schedule we have no time to sleep
due to our workload and we don’t have
proper track on our sleep.So we are
motivated by our daily lifestyle and
took up the task to develop a sleep monitoring app.

Meditate Now

Meditation slows down your heart rate and lowers
the levels of the stress hormone cortisol in your body,
two things that happen naturally when you sleep. Meditation
can also cause you to have theta brainwaves, the same state
your brain enters when you are falling asleep
